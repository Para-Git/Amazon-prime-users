Business Problems:

1. Churn Prediction – Why are some users canceling their subscriptions?  

2. User Engagement – How does engagement vary across different subscription plans?  

3. Device Preference – Which devices do users prefer for streaming, and how does it impact retention?  

4. Genre Popularity – What are the most popular content genres among different age groups?  

5. Customer Support Impact – Does interacting with customer support affect a user’s churn rate?  

6. Subscription Plan Analysis – Which plan (monthly or annual) is more popular among different user segments?  

7. Feedback & Ratings – How do user ratings relate to churn and engagement levels?
